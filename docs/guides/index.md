# Guides Hub

Welcome to the markata-go documentation! Choose a learning path based on your goals, or browse individual guides below.

---

## Learning Paths

### Beginner Path
**Time: ~30 minutes** | Get your first site running

Perfect if you're new to markata-go or static site generators in general.

| Step | Guide | Time | Description |
|------|-------|------|-------------|
| 1 | [Getting Started](/docs/getting-started/) | 10 min | Installation and first site |
| 2 | [Quickstart](/docs/quickstart/) | 5 min | Build and serve your site |
| 3 | [Frontmatter](/docs/guides/frontmatter/) | 10 min | Understanding post metadata |
| 4 | [Markdown Features](/docs/guides/markdown/) | 5 min | Writing content with Markdown |

**After completing:** You'll have a working site with your first posts.

---

### Blogger Path
**Time: ~45 minutes** | Create a polished blog

For writers and content creators who want a beautiful, functional blog.

| Step | Guide | Time | Description |
|------|-------|------|-------------|
| 1 | [Getting Started](/docs/getting-started/) | 10 min | Installation and setup |
| 2 | [Themes and Styling](/docs/guides/themes/) | 10 min | Customize your site's look |
| 3 | [Feeds](/docs/guides/feeds/) | 10 min | RSS, archives, and tag pages |
| 4 | [Syndication Feeds](/docs/guides/syndication-feeds/) | 5 min | RSS/Atom configuration |
| 5 | [Deployment](/docs/guides/deployment/) | 10 min | Go live on GitHub Pages, Netlify, etc. |

**After completing:** You'll have a deployed blog with RSS feeds and custom styling.
