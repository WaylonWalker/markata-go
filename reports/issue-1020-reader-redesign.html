Issue #1020

Reader redesign

Date rail, columns, and source branding for /reader/, demonstrated against the real waylonwalker.com blogroll configuration.

What changed

  • Grouped reader entries by day.
  • Added a large date rail and two-column desktop layout.
  • Moved source favicons to the front of each entry.
  • Reworked preview fallbacks to prefer article media, then source images, then screenshots, then branded source tiles.
  • Kept pagination and mobile collapse behavior intact.
